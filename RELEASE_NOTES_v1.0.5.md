## ğŸ“– Getting Started

### Quick Start Guide

1. **Add a playlist:**
   - Click "Playlists" tab
   - Upload M3U file or paste URL
   - Or try Pluto TV for instant free channels

2. **Set up TV Guide:**
   - Go to "EPG Settings"
   - Add EPG XML URL
   - Assign to specific playlists
   - Click "Refresh EPG Data"

3. **Start watching:**
   - Go to "Live TV"
   - Browse or search for channels
   - Click to play
   - Press F for fullscreen
